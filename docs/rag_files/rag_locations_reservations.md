## MAKING A RESERVATION

### Reservation Process

**Information We Need:**
1. **Guest Name** - Name for the reservation
2. **Preferred Branch** - BGC, Pasig, or Quezon City
3. **Date** - When you'd like to dine with us
4. **Time** - Your preferred dining time
5. **Party Size** - Number of guests in your group
6. **Contact Number** - For confirmation and any updates

### How to Reserve

**Phone Reservations:**
- Call any of our numbers: (02) 8662-2523 or 0917 524 5058
- Available during operating hours
- Speak directly with our staff

**Email Reservations:**
- General: ask@bistro.com.ph
- Branch-specific emails for direct contact
- Include all reservation details in your email
- We'll confirm within 24 hours

**Walk-ins:**
- Always welcome!
- Subject to availability
- Reservations recommended for:
  - Weekend dinners (Friday-Saturday evenings)
  - Large groups (6+ people)
  - Special occasions
  - Peak lunch hours (12:00-2:00 PM)

### Reservation Confirmation

After booking, we will:
1. **Confirm your details** - We'll repeat back your reservation information
2. **Provide branch details** - Address, parking info, and directions if needed
3. **Send confirmation** - Via phone call, SMS, or email
4. **Remind you** - We may send a courtesy reminder before your reservation

### Reservation Policies

**Arrival Time:**
- Please arrive within 15 minutes of your reservation time
- If running late, please call us to hold your table
- Tables are held for 15 minutes past reservation time

**Large Groups (8+ people):**
- Advance booking required (minimum 24 hours)
- Special arrangements can be made for parties of 12+
- Ask about private dining options
- Pre-ordering available for large groups

**Special Requests:**
- Birthdays, anniversaries, celebrations - let us know!
- Dietary restrictions - inform us in advance
- Seating preferences - we'll do our best to accommodate
- Special decorations - available for celebrations

**Cancellations & Changes:**
- Please give us at least 2 hours notice
- Call the branch directly or use your confirmation contact
- We understand plans change - just let us know!

## GETTING THERE

### Driving Directions

**To BGC:**
- From EDSA: Take C5 to McKinley Parkway, enter BGC via 5th Ave
- From South: SLEX to Skyway, exit at Buendia, follow to BGC
- From North: EDSA to McKinley Road to BGC

**To Pasig (Estancia):**
- From EDSA: Take Ortigas Ave Extension to Camino Verde
- From C5: Exit at Ortigas Extension
- From Shaw Blvd: Follow to Capitol Commons area

**To QC (Opus):**
- From EDSA: Take Ortigas Ave to E. Rodriguez Jr. Ave
- From C5: Exit at E. Rodriguez Jr. Ave
- From Katipunan: Follow to E. Rodriguez Jr. Ave

### Public Transportation

**BGC:**
- BGC Bus from EDSA (Ayala or Guadalupe stations)
- Jeepneys along Kalayaan Avenue

**Pasig:**

- Jeepneys along Ortigas Avenue

- Jeepneys along E. Rodriguez Jr. Avenue
- Accessible from various QC routes
