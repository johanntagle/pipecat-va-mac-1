# TOMATITO SEXY TAPAS BAR - CHEF'S RECOMMENDATIONS

## STANDOUT DISHES - CHEF'S PICKS

### Patatas Bravas
**The Spanish Classic Done Right**
Perfectly crispy golden potatoes served with two signature sauces that define this beloved dish.

**What Makes Ours Special:**
- Double-fried for maximum crispiness
- Exterior: Golden and crunchy
- Interior: Fluffy and tender
- Drizzled with house-made chili oil for heat
- Finished with creamy aioli for balance

**Flavor Profile:** Spicy, creamy, crispy
**Heat Level:** Medium (can be adjusted)
**Serving Size:** Perfect for 2-3 people
**Dietary Notes:** Vegetarian
**Perfect Pairing:** Spanish beer or sangria
**Why The Chef Loves It:** "It's the perfect balance of textures and temperatures - every bite is different!"

### Gambas al Ajillo
**Sizzling Garlic Shrimp**
A quintessential Spanish tapa that arrives at your table still sizzling in a traditional clay pot.

**Preparation:**
- Fresh, plump shrimp
- Abundant garlic slices (not minced - for sweeter, milder flavor)
- White wine for depth
- Spanish olive oil base
- Touch of chili for warmth
- Fresh parsley finish

**Flavor Profile:** Garlicky, buttery, aromatic
**Serving Temperature:** Served hot and sizzling
**Bread Recommendation:** Essential! Use our crusty bread to soak up the incredible garlic-infused oil
**Perfect Pairing:** Crisp white wine or Spanish vermouth
**Chef's Tip:** "Don't be shy with the bread - the oil is the best part!"

### Sexy Chicken Fingers
**Not Your Average Chicken Strips**
Elevated comfort food with a Spanish twist. These aren't the chicken fingers you remember from childhood.

**What Makes Them Sexy:**
- Premium chicken breast, hand-cut
- Spanish-spiced breading with paprika and herbs
- Perfectly fried to golden perfection
- Served with house-made honey mustard sauce

**Flavor Profile:** Savory, slightly sweet, crispy
**Sauce:** Honey mustard with a Spanish kick
**Best For:** Those who love comfort food with sophistication
**Perfect Pairing:** Spanish beer or light cocktails
**Kid-Friendly:** Absolutely, but adults love them too!

### Carpaccio de Solomillo
**Beef Carpaccio with Luxury Touches**
Paper-thin slices of premium beef tenderloin, elevated to art on a plate.

**Key Components:**
- Premium beef tenderloin, sliced paper-thin
- Truffle sour cream for earthy richness
- Fresh jalapeño slices for heat and brightness
- Microgreens and finishing oil
- Parmesan shavings

**Flavor Profile:** Rich, earthy, spicy, umami
**Temperature:** Served chilled
**Texture:** Melt-in-your-mouth tender
**Heat Level:** Mild to medium (jalapeño can be adjusted)
**Perfect Pairing:** Full-bodied red wine or aged sherry
**Chef's Philosophy:** "Quality ingredients need little intervention - we let the beef shine"

## DIETARY ACCOMMODATIONS
- Vegetarian options: Patatas Bravas
- Gluten-conscious diners: Ask about modifications for most dishes
- Spice levels: Can be adjusted on most items
- Allergies: Please inform your server - we take them seriously

## ORDERING SUGGESTIONS
- These dishes work beautifully as a progression
- Start with Patatas Bravas or Carpaccio
- Follow with Gambas al Ajillo (don't forget extra bread!)
- Finish with Sexy Chicken Fingers for something hearty
- Perfect for sharing among 3-4 people
