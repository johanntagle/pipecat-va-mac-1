# TOMATITO SEXY TAPAS BAR - TAPAS & PINTXOS

## SEXY BITES - SIGNATURE SMALL PLATES

### Bombas de Jamon (Ham Bombs)
**The Crowd Favorite**
Crispy exterior meets creamy interior in these irresistible spheres. Filled with Manchego cheese cream and a touch of smoky paprika, each bomb delivers an explosion of Spanish flavors.

**Flavor Profile:** Creamy, smoky, savory
**Best For:** Sharing, starting your meal
**Option Available:** Add extra pieces for a bigger flavor experience
**Perfect Pairing:** Spanish beer or light white wine

### Tuna TNT
**A Spicy Sensation**
Fresh tuna tataki prepared with precision and finished with a spicy twist that gives it the "TNT" name. This dish showcases the fusion of Japanese technique with Spanish boldness.

**Flavor Profile:** Fresh, spicy, umami-rich
**Preparation:** Lightly seared, served rare
**Heat Level:** Medium-high
**Perfect Pairing:** Crisp white wine or sake
**Option Available:** Add extra pieces

### Mini Pork Buns
**Asian-Spanish Fusion at its Finest**
Soft, pillowy steamed buns filled with succulent seasoned pork. A nod to international flavors while maintaining our Spanish soul.

**Flavor Profile:** Savory, slightly sweet, tender
**Serving Size:** Perfect bite-sized portions
**Best For:** Adventurous eaters, sharing plates
**Option Available:** Add extra pieces

### Chorizo and Manchego Air Baguette
**Light Yet Flavorful**
Crispy air-light baguette topped with premium Spanish chorizo and aged Manchego cheese. The airy texture of the bread contrasts beautifully with the rich toppings.

**Flavor Profile:** Smoky, nutty, crispy
**Key Ingredients:** 
- Spanish chorizo (authentic paprika-spiced)
- Aged Manchego cheese
- House-made air baguette
**Perfect Pairing:** Spanish red wine or vermouth
**Option Available:** Add extra pieces

## ORDERING TIPS
- Most tapas are designed for sharing among 2-4 people
- Order 2-3 tapas per person for a full meal experience
- Mix and match different flavor profiles for the best experience
- Extra pieces available on most items - just ask!

## THE TAPAS EXPERIENCE
Tapas at Tomatito are meant to be enjoyed slowly, shared generously, and paired with great conversation. Order multiple small plates to experience the variety of Spanish flavors and textures.
