# TOMATITO SEXY TAPAS BAR - PAELLAS

## THE ART OF PAELLA

Paella is more than just a dish - it's a Spanish tradition that brings people together. At Tomatito, each paella is prepared to order in traditional paellera pans, ensuring the authentic socarrat (crispy bottom layer) that defines a perfectly made paella.

**Cooking Time:** 25-30 minutes (made fresh to order)
**Serving Size:** Each paella serves 2-3 people generously
**Best Enjoyed:** Family-style, shared from the pan at the table

## OUR PAELLA SELECTION

### Paella Iberica
**The Pride of the Peninsula**
A meat lover's dream featuring the finest Iberian pork products that Spain is famous for.

**Star Ingredients:**
- Premium Iberian pork (from acorn-fed pigs)
- Iberian ham (jamón) for depth of flavor
- Spanish bomba rice (absorbs flavor perfectly)
- Saffron-infused broth
- Seasonal vegetables

**Flavor Profile:** Rich, savory, deeply porky
**Character:** Hearty and satisfying
**Perfect For:** Red meat lovers, those seeking authentic Spanish flavors
**Wine Pairing:** Robust Spanish red wine (Rioja or Ribera del Duero)
**Chef's Note:** "This showcases the crown jewels of Spanish charcuterie"

### Paella Negra (Black Paella)
**A Dramatic Seafood Masterpiece**
Named for its striking black color from squid ink, this is a seafood lover's adventure.

**Key Components:**
- Baby squid (tender and sweet)
- Squid ink (for color, briny flavor, and drama)
- Mixed seafood selection
- Rich, creamy aioli sauce on the side
- Bomba rice infused with seafood stock

**Flavor Profile:** Briny, oceanic, rich, slightly sweet
**Visual Appeal:** Stunning jet-black presentation
**Texture:** Creamy rice with tender seafood
**Perfect For:** Adventurous eaters, seafood enthusiasts
**Wine Pairing:** Crisp white wine or cava
**Did You Know:** The squid ink adds umami depth and a subtle sea flavor
**Serving Suggestion:** Mix the aioli into the rice for extra creaminess

### Paella El Chiringuito
**Beach Shack Seafood Classic**
Named after the casual beach restaurants of Spain's coast, this seafood paella captures the essence of Spanish seaside dining.

**Fresh Seafood Selection:**
- Large prawns/shrimp
- Fresh mussels
- Clams
- Squid
- Seasonal fish
- Saffron-infused seafood broth

**Flavor Profile:** Fresh, seafood-forward, aromatic
**Inspiration:** Spanish beach culture and chiringuito dining
**Character:** Light yet satisfying
**Perfect For:** Classic seafood paella lovers
**Wine Pairing:** Albariño, Verdejo, or Spanish rosé
**Chef's Tip:** "We source the freshest seafood daily for this dish"

### Paella Cochinillo (Suckling Pig Paella)
**The Grand Celebration Dish**
Our most luxurious paella, featuring the prized cochinillo (suckling pig) that's a delicacy in Spanish cuisine.

**Special Features:**
- Tender, milk-fed suckling pig
- Crispy skin pieces incorporated
- Rich, porky cooking liquid
- Traditional Castilian preparation meets Valencian rice technique
- Serves 3-4 people (larger portion)

**Flavor Profile:** Luxurious, rich, crispy-tender contrast
**Occasion:** Special celebrations, when you want to impress
**Preparation:** Requires extra time - please allow 35-40 minutes
**Price Point:** Premium (reflects the quality of the suckling pig)
**Wine Pairing:** Aged Rioja or premium Tempranillo
**Why It's Special:** Cochinillo is a protected delicacy in Spain, traditionally roasted in wood ovens
**Perfect For:** Special occasions, adventurous gourmands, groups wanting something extraordinary

## THE TOMATITO PAELLA DIFFERENCE

**Authentic Technique:**
- Traditional paellera pans for even cooking
- Bomba rice from Valencia (the paella capital)
- Real saffron threads (not substitutes)
- Made to order - never pre-made
- Achieving the perfect socarrat (crispy bottom)

**What is Socarrat?**
The caramelized, crispy layer of rice at the bottom of the pan - considered the best part of paella. We ensure every paella has this prized element.

## PAELLA DINING ETIQUETTE

**Traditional Way:**
- Eat directly from the pan using wooden spoons
- Work from the outside in, moving toward the center
- Share with your dining companions
- Save the socarrat for last (or fight for it!)

**Modern Way:**
We can also serve individual portions if preferred

## ORDERING TIPS

- Order paellas at the start of your meal (25-30 minute cooking time)
- Start with tapas and drinks while your paella cooks
- One paella + 2-3 tapas = perfect meal for 3-4 people
- Can't decide? Ask your server about the day's freshest seafood or chef's recommendation
